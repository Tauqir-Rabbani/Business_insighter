About Lowe's

Lucius Smith Lowe founded the North Wilkesboro Hardware Store in North Carolina in 1921. After he passed away, his kids re-named the store Lowe's in his honor. Since then, Lowe's has become one of the leading hardware stores for your home needs with over 2,000 stores. They offer a variety of products like tools, appliances, outdoor decor, home decor, and more.

Whether you're renovating, starting a DIY project or need new equipment, Lowe's has all of your home improvement needs. Find huge savings during holidays like Christmas, Fourth of July, and Memorial Day and stack your savings with a Lowe's promo code. Lowe's knows how to help you improve your home at a great price.
